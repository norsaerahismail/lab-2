Self-love

Self-love is a state of appreciation for oneself that involves caring for your physical, psychological, and spiritual needs, and includes treating yourself with kindness, acceptance, and compassion, even during difficult times. It means recognizing your own worth, setting boundaries, and prioritizing your well-being without being selfish or narcissistic. It is a fundamental part of self-confidence and mental well-being that is developed through a consistent, lifelong practice.
